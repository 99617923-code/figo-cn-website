广州火鹰信息科技有限公司（简称火鹰科技，股权编号890461）创始于2005年，是国内领先的「互联网+」技术服务商，国家高新技术企业，业务涉及商用高端APP定制开发、微信小程序开发、AR/VR、政务系统、产品孵化及技术创投等领域，目前与多家企业达成合作伙伴关系。
火鹰科技创始人来自中山大学计算机专业，作为广东省大学生创业典型，多次获得国家、省市级领导接见，被国内近20家电视台、报纸媒体争相报道。至今，火鹰科技服务过上市公司、大型国企、各类私企超1000家，为多家公司提供各类政务系统、app开发定制、微信小程序开发定制、智能家居、直播+、电商系统、人脉共享、知识付费问答、语音红包等技术解决方案服务。
同时，火鹰科技在智能硬件方面、区块链应用方面持续投入关注及创新。
Guangzhou FIGO Information Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 2005.Since 2015, it has been rated as a National High-tech Enterprise for three consecutive sessions. It is a leading "Internet +" technology service provider in China. Its business covers the fields of smart buildings, smart parks, the Internet of Things, customized development of high-end commercial APPs, development of WeChat mini-programs, construction of medium and large-scale information systems, product incubation, etc.
So far, FIGO Technology has served more than 2,000 listed companies, large state-owned enterprises and various private enterprises. It has provided technical services such as the development of various government affairs information systems, intelligent software and hardware systems, the development of industrial Internet platforms, and the construction of new energy vehicle charging system projects for many companies.
